# GD Requests

Browse and manage your level request queue without ever leaving Geometry Dash.

## Features

- **Live queue** -- see all pending requests from your viewers in-game
- **Jump to level** -- tap any request to instantly search for that level in GD
- **Remove / Ban** -- delete or blacklist a request directly from the popup
- **Remove All** -- clear your entire queue with one button (with confirmation)
- **Pause menu actions** -- Remove or Ban the current level mid-run from the pause screen
- **Auto-mark as played** -- entering a requested level automatically marks it Done on your dashboard
- **Now Playing toast** -- a notification shows who requested the level when it loads
- **F2 hotkey** -- press F2 anywhere in GD to open your queue instantly
- **YouTube showcase** -- open a requester's showcase video in your browser with one tap
- **Real-time sync** -- your gdrequests.org dashboard updates automatically as you play
- **Blackscreen toggle** -- Toggle to black out the screen to prevent nsfw jumpscares from nuking streamers (by MalikHw47) (mobile support added)

## Setup

1. Register at [gdrequests.org](https://www.gdrequests.org)
2. Go to **Geode Integration** in your dashboard and copy your token
3. Paste it into the **Creator Token** field in this mod's settings
4. Click the GD Requests icon in the top-right corner of the main menu, or press **F2**
